\section{Other interesting tips}\label{other-interesting-tips}}

You can print values of R variables in text.

The average body mass in grams of all penguins is 4201.754386. You can
also use \texttt{round} and \texttt{scales::comma} to make it
better-looking, so that it prints like so: the average body mass in
grams of all penguins is 4,202.
